Chicken Karahi

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken 1 KG

  • Cocking Oil

  • Tomato Half KG

  • Yogurd 250 G

  • Ginger Garlic

  • Red Crushed Chilli

  • Green Chilli

  • Red Chilli Powder

  • Black Paper

  • Dhania

  • Haldi

  • Salt

RECIPE

  1. Get a karahi and fry Chicken in Salt for 10 minuts
  2. Add Ginger Garlic Paste and fry 2 minuts
  3. Add Tomatos and cock until tomato water dry
  4. Add Salt , Chilli Powder , Haldi , Crushed Chilli And mix
  5. Add Yogurd Dry Yogurd water
  6. Add Black Papper , Green Chilli , Dhania And mix
  7. Now Garnish With Dhania , Ginger And EAT
